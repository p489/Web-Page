ABOUT NCC IITB

The NCC in India was formed the National Cadet Corps Act of 1948.Being one of the only two engineering regiments in India, 2 Maharashtra Engineering Regiment is a National Cadet Corps unit under the aegis of Bombay Engineer Group. NCC IIT Bombay tend to developing patriotism, brotherhood and discipline in the cadets, thereby creating dynamic and visionary leaders of future India.

HISTORY OF THE UNIT

1964 :Raised as no.1 MAH ENGR REGT.NCC.
1965 :Redesignated as No.4 MAH ENGR COY.NCC.
1968 :Redesignated as 1 MAH MIXED REGT NCC DEC VICTORIA JUBILEE TECHNICAL INSTITUTE.
1969 :Redesignated as 2 MAH ENGR REGT NCC.
1994 :REGT Placed under suspended animation.
2005 :Efforts for revival started wef.
2006 :First Parade after suspended animation started.
